2339Redstone Soup?Redstone soup is a witches brew that forms the base for all infusions, although it does have other uses.

Filling a chalice with redstone soup will increase its magical affinity and upgrade it to a filled chalice, doubling its effectiveness.

Drinking redstone soup will boost the health of the drinker for a few minutes.

Redstone Soup is made in a kettle.

2358Visit the Enchanted IslandA very flat biome with small amount of caves.

It can be found with the HEE Biome Compass.

There are two variations - Homeland and Laboratory.

Homeland has lakes of Ender Goo, piles of Falling Obsidian and a strange Obsidian road-like structure.

You can often find Homeland Cache, a floating transparent cube that can be broken. Endermen Baby Endermen and Ender Guardians spawn there.

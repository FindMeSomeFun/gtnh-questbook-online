There are lots of keybinds in this modpack, to avoid conflicts most of them are unbinded by default and only those available from the start of the game are binded.
Here is a list of what some of them do :


BetterQuesting
GRAVE - Open Questbook

Battlegear
R - Draw Weapons - It's a second hotbar that holds weapons

Draconic Evolution
V - Place the item you are holding on the block you are looking at

Journeymap
B - Create a Waypoint
J - Open Fullscreen Map
= - Zoom In (works on minimap too)
- - Zoom out

Inventory Tweaks
R - Sort Inventory

Share Where I am
INSERT - Places a temporary beam where you are looking at that will be seen by you and other players
SHIFT + INSERT - Places a temporary beam where you are that will be seen by you and other players
CTRL + INSERT - Shares with other players the position you are looking at
CTRL + SHIFT + INSERT - Shares with other players your position

Waila - Shows informations of what you are looking at
NUMPAD1 - show/hide the Waila HUD
NUMPAD3 - shows how to craft what you are looking at
NUMPAD4 - shows the crafting usages of the thing you are looking at

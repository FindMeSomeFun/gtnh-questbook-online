2382Infused AltarAltar with one or more special effects (enhancements).

The way to make an enhanced altar is to right click a Brewing Stand with End Powder in your Hand. You can continue adding as many effects to one Altar as you want.

Enhancements:
Range (Block of Lapis)
Increase the range a item can be infused by the Altar.

Speed (Block of Redstone)
Increasing the speed of the Altar.

Efficency (Block of Emerald)
